We are a data analytics team dedicated to uncovering patterns of bias in the social development of India. We are investigating bias themes related to corporate influence on policy making, the political economy, role of the mass media in influencing public opinion, and inequality in the development of different regions as observed using data from censuses and satellite imagery.
